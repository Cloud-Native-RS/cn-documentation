# OpenShift Deployment

This section provides step-by-step guidance for planning, deploying, and enabling your organization on **Red Hat OpenShift**.  
The deployment process is structured to ensure **security, scalability, and operational excellence**.

---

## Recommended Page Structure & Order

### 1. **Overview**
- Introduction to OpenShift deployment models (Bare Metal, Virtualized, Cloud, Hybrid).  
- Key considerations: scale, compliance, integrations.  
- Deployment goals and success criteria.  

---

### 2. **Infrastructure and Capacity**
- Infrastructure requirements and sizing guidelines.  
- Capacity planning and resource allocation strategies.  
- Hardware specifications and performance optimization.  
- Infrastructure validation and testing procedures.  

---

### 3. **Installation & Setup**
- Choosing the right installation method:  
  - Installer-Provisioned Infrastructure (IPI).  
  - User-Provisioned Infrastructure (UPI).  
- Step-by-step installation guides.  
- Bootstrap process, cluster initialization.  
- Verification of successful installation.  

---

### 4. **Cluster Configuration**
- Configuring Operators & OperatorHub.  
- Setting up authentication and RBAC.  
- Storage and persistent volumes.  
- Ingress, routing, and networking policies.  

---

### 5. **Security Hardening**
- Applying baseline security policies.  
- Integrating with enterprise IAM (LDAP, SSO, OAuth).  
- Pod security, namespaces, and multi-tenancy.  
- Compliance & audit logging.  

---

### 6. **Automation & GitOps**
- Automating deployments with Ansible & Helm.  
- GitOps with ArgoCD.  
- CI/CD pipelines with Tekton.  
- Infrastructure-as-Code (IaC) templates.  

---

### 7. **Day-2 Operations Enablement**
- Monitoring & observability (Prometheus, Grafana, EFK/ELK).  
- Backup & disaster recovery setup.  
- Scaling clusters and applications.  
- Routine maintenance tasks.  

---

### 8. **Migration & Modernization**
- Migrating workloads from VMware or legacy infrastructure.  
- Containerizing existing applications.  
- Best practices for workload mobility.  

---

### 9. **Training & Team Enablement**
- Hands-on labs for administrators and developers.  
- Documentation for self-service and onboarding.  
- Recommended certification paths (RHCSA, OpenShift Admin/Dev).  

---

### 10. **Checklists & Resources**
- ✅ Deployment readiness checklist.  
- ✅ Post-deployment validation checklist.  
- ✅ Operations & monitoring checklist.  
- Links to Red Hat resources, playbooks, and community docs.
